2. Create `src/lib/ai-engine.ts` as a pure module with two exported functions:

**`scoreBids(bids: Bid[], suppliers: Supplier[], rfp: RFP): ScoredBid[]`**

Scoring formulas (all clamped 0-100):
- `price`: `Math.max(0, Math.min(100, (1 - bid.amount / rfp.budgetCeiling) * 100))`
- `delivery`: `Math.max(0, Math.min(100, (1 - bid.deliveryDays / 365) * 100))`
- `reliability`: `supplier.pastPerformance` (already 0-100)
- `compliance`: `(supplier.complianceStatus === 'Compliant' ? 60 : supplier.complianceStatus === 'Pending Review' ? 30 : 0) + Math.min(40, supplier.certifications.length * 10)`
- `risk`: `Math.max(0, 100 - supplier.riskScore)` (already 0-100 scale)

Weights: `{ price: 0.30, delivery: 0.20, reliability: 0.25, compliance: 0.15, risk: 0.10 }`

Composite: `Math.round(scores.price * 0.30 + scores.delivery * 0.20 + scores.reliability * 0.25 + scores.compliance * 0.15 + scores.risk * 0.10)`

Sort descending by composite. Assign `rank` (1-based). Mark `isWinner: true` only on rank 1.

For each bid, find the matching supplier by `bid.supplierId === supplier.id`. If supplier not found, skip that bid.

**`generateNarrative(ranked: ScoredBid[]): string`**

Template: Find winner (rank 1) and runner-up (rank 2). Identify winner's top dimension (highest score). Identify runner-up's weakest dimension (lowest score). Compute gap = winner.composite - runnerUp.composite.

Return: `"Based on ${winner.supplier.name}'s strong ${topDimension} score of ${topDimensionValue}/100 and competitive pricing at $${winner.bid.amount.toLocaleString()}, they represent the best value. ${runnerUp.supplier.name} scored ${gap} points lower, primarily due to ${weakestDimension}."`

Export both functions and the WEIGHTS constant.
